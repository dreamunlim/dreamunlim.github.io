About game

Dreamer Unlimited is a simple casual web game created for lulz. The main objective of which is to catch stuff falling, namely hearts, stars, booster and, optionally, friends to score points.

Game session is fast-paced and lasts ten seconds. Picking up the booster dice will recharge the timer. The spiders are the only ones to avoid. Colliding with them has a chance to end the game. Thus, you will have to think and move fast to take a strategically advantageous lane. Game controls are intuitive. Tap either on the left or right screen part to move hero left or right, or use keyboard arrows and Esc to pause. Moving hero into the screen edge teleports him to the opposite side. If you have a minute to spare before doing something awesome, give the Dream Unlim game a shot!

Game features

To have a good laugh with your mates, you can:

  • Override both heart pictures with a choice of your own, for example, with pictures of your friends
  • Share your best score with Facebook friends
