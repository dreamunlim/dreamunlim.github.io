About game

Dreamer Unlimited is a simple casual web game where you catch falling objects, namely hearts, stars, diamond, booster and, optionally, friends to score points.

How to play

Tap either on the left or right screen part to move hero left or right, or use keyboard arrows and Esc to pause. Moving hero into the screen edge teleports him to the opposite side. Game session is fast-paced and lasts ten seconds. Catch the stuff falling to score points. Pick up the booster dice to recharge the timer. The spiders are the only ones to avoid. Colliding with them has a chance to end the game.

Game features

  • Three characters to select from
  • Overriding both heart pictures with a choice of your own, for example, with pictures of your friends
  • Sharing your best score with Facebook friends
